# Handicap Rules & Odds Reference | 盘口规则与赔率参考

Settlement rules for every market this skill covers, the Chinese handicap
terminology glossary, and intuition tables linking expected-goals supremacy to
fair lines. **Never settle a handicap or convert a line mentally — the rules
below exist so you can recognize and sanity-check markets; all arithmetic goes
through `scripts/predict.py` and `scripts/value.py`.**

## 1. European 1X2 | 欧赔胜平负

Three outcomes: home win / draw / away win (胜/平/负). Decimal odds. Implied
probability = 1/odds; the sum exceeds 1 by the bookmaker margin (vig, 水钱).
`value.py` removes the vig with both proportional and power methods and uses
the power method for decisions (it corrects favourite-longshot bias).

## 2. Asian Handicap | 亚洲让球盘

Two outcomes plus pushes. The line (盘口) is added to the backed team's goals;
the adjusted margin settles the bet. Prices are usually quoted as HK odds
(水位, typically 0.75–1.15); decimal ≈ HK + 1.

### Settlement by line type

| Line type | Example | Settlement |
|---|---|---|
| Integer | −1 | Win if margin > 1; **push (stake returned)** if margin = 1; lose otherwise |
| Half | −0.5 | Win if margin ≥ 1; lose otherwise. No push possible |
| Quarter | −0.75 | Stake splits in half across the two adjacent lines (−0.5 and −1). Outcomes: full win / **half win** / **half lose** / full lose |

### Quarter-ball outcomes (the part people get wrong)

Backing home −0.75, stake 100:
- Home wins by 2+: both halves win → full win
- Home wins by exactly 1: −0.5 half wins, −1 half pushes → **win half** (赢半)
- Draw or home loses: both halves lose → full lose

Backing home −0.25, stake 100:
- Home wins: full win
- Draw: −0 half pushes, −0.5 half loses → **lose half** (输半)
- Home loses: full lose

`predict.py` outputs the full five-way distribution
(`full_win / half_win / push / half_lose / full_lose`) for every line from
−2.0 to +2.0 in quarter steps, for both sides independently.

### Chinese line terminology | 盘口术语表

| 中文 | Line | 中文 | Line |
|---|---|---|---|
| 平手 | 0 | 一球 | −1.0 |
| 平半 / 平手半球 | −0.25 | 一球一球半 / 一球球半 | −1.25 |
| 半球 | −0.5 | 球半 | −1.5 |
| 半一 / 半球一球 | −0.75 | 球半两球 | −1.75 |
| | | 两球 | −2.0 |

受让 (receiving) prefixes flip the sign: 受让半球 = +0.5. The favourite
(让球方) gives the ball; identify which team is giving **by team name, never
by row position** in a screenshot.

## 3. China Sports Lottery 让球胜平负 | CSL handicap 3-way

Integer handicap applied to the home team's goals, then settled as a normal
3-way (win/draw/lose) — **the draw is a real outcome**, unlike Asian handicap
where integer lines push. 主让一球 means handicap = −1: home 2-0 becomes 1-0
(让球胜); home 1-0 becomes 0-0 (让球平). Fixed decimal odds, no water levels,
no pushes, no half wins.

Also relevant for parlays: CSL 串关 (N串1, N串M) multiplies decimal odds
across matches — see `value.py --parlay`.

## 4. Double chance | 双重机会

Three overlapping covers over the 1X2 outcomes, each a two-in-three bet:

| Cover | Wins if | 中文 |
|---|---|---|
| 1X | home win or draw | 主胜或平 / 主不败 |
| 12 | home win or away win (no draw) | 主胜或客胜 / 不平 |
| X2 | draw or away win | 平或客胜 / 客不败 |

Because the covers overlap, their probabilities do **not** sum to 1 —
`predict.py` derives each directly from the 1X2 vector (1X = home + draw, etc.)
and prints fair odds per cover. There are no pushes and no water levels; settle
as a simple win/lose on whether the covered result occurred. This is the
highest-hit-rate market the skill prices, which is exactly why the
confidence-first objective leans on it — see `value.py --objective confidence`.

## 5. Odds format recognition | 赔率格式识别

| Format | Range signature | Decimal conversion |
|---|---|---|
| Decimal (欧洲盘) | ≥ 1.01, favourites ~1.2–2.5 | as-is |
| HK (香港盘/水位) | mostly 0.75–1.15, always > 0 | +1 |
| Malay (马来盘) | −1..1, positive and negative mixed | pos: +1; neg: 1+1/\|v\| |
| Indo (印尼盘) | \|v\| ≥ 1, negative favourites | pos: +1; neg: 1+1/\|v\| |

Values between 1.0 and 1.25 are **ambiguous** (decimal vs HK) — `value.py`
refuses to guess and demands `--odds-format`. Respect that; a wrong format
assumption silently corrupts every downstream number.

## 6. Intuition tables

> Generated by `predict.py v1.0.0` (ρ = −0.10, total ≈ 2.6 goals).

### Supremacy ↔ fair Asian handicap line

| Supremacy (λh−λa) | λh / λa | Home win | Draw | Away win | Fair AH line (home) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| −1.00 | 0.80 / 1.80 | 15% | 25% | 60% | +1.00 |
| −0.50 | 1.05 / 1.55 | 24% | 28% | 48% | +0.50 |
| −0.25 | 1.18 / 1.43 | 30% | 29% | 42% | +0.25 |
| +0.00 | 1.30 / 1.30 | 36% | 29% | 36% | +0.00 |
| +0.25 | 1.43 / 1.18 | 42% | 29% | 30% | −0.25 |
| +0.50 | 1.55 / 1.05 | 48% | 28% | 24% | −0.50 |
| +0.75 | 1.68 / 0.93 | 54% | 27% | 20% | −0.75 |
| +1.00 | 1.80 / 0.80 | 60% | 25% | 15% | −1.00 |
| +1.25 | 1.93 / 0.68 | 66% | 23% | 11% | −1.25 |
| +1.50 | 2.05 / 0.55 | 72% | 20% | 8% | −1.50 |
| +2.00 | 2.30 / 0.30 | 82% | 15% | 3% | −2.00 |

Read it backwards too: a market line of 半一 (−0.75) at even water implies the
market believes supremacy ≈ +0.75. If your derived λs imply +0.2, either you
know something the market doesn't (write the thesis down) or you missed
something (more likely — re-examine).

### CSL handicap intuition

| λh / λa | h=0 (胜/平/负) | h=−1 (让一球) |
|---|---|---|
| 2.20 / 0.80 | 68% / 20% / 11% | 45% / 23% / 32% |
| 1.90 / 1.00 | 57% / 24% / 18% | 34% / 23% / 43% |
| 1.55 / 1.03 | 48% / 28% / 24% | 25% / 23% / 52% |
| 1.30 / 1.30 | 36% / 29% / 36% | 16% / 19% / 64% |
| 1.00 / 1.60 | 22% / 27% / 50% | 8% / 14% / 78% |

Note how brutal 让一球 is: even a strong favourite (57% straight win) covers
−1 only 34% of the time. Giving a goal in a 3-way market demands roughly a
2-goal supremacy to be the likeliest outcome.
